Setting up Atomic Docs with Less preprocessor is pretty easy, but comes with one caveat.

Caveat

By convention, LESS partials are not prefixed with an underscore like Sass partials are. In Atomic Docs, at least for the moment, all partials need to have the underscore whether Sass or Less.

Steps

  • open up atomic-config.php and change $config['preCssDir'] to "less". Also change $config['preCssExt'] to "less".
